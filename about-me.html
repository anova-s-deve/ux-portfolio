    About me

    Hi again! My name is Anova S. Deve. I worked in maturing roles for developmental and humanitarian non-profits before moving to a technology start-up in the capacity of a UI/UX designer and have since been on this path learning more.

    I stumbled upon the field when my brother asked me if I could design a marketing website for a restaurant customer he had at the time. I only recall thinking that if I tried, this should be something I could do. But I don't recall making that website! However, the outcome of this request, after a rabid online learning spree, was an unsolicited UX case study which lead me to my first UI/UX position.

    I have had the opportunity to work on both, consumer UX products in start-ups and now on an enterprise product, with a bigger team. I feel passionate about both as they have equally challenging design problems as long as they are in organisations that value work-life balance. :)

    On a more personal note, I am a mindfulness enthusiast and deeply care about the mental health of those around me. I love reading emails sent by friends and replying to them. So feel free to drop me one!
